# Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to decide whether a job is worth applying to and how aggressively the resume should be tailored.

## Gate Checks

Check these before scoring. If any critical gate fails, cap the final recommendation at `不合适` unless the user explicitly wants a stretch plan.

| Gate | Critical miss examples |
|---|---|
| Location / work mode | Impossible location, non-negotiable on-site mismatch |
| Required credential | Required license, degree, clearance, or language level missing |
| Required seniority | Role asks for staff/principal scope but resume shows only entry-level scope |
| Required domain | Role requires regulated or specialized domain experience and no adjacent evidence exists |
| Required technical foundation | Core stack or capability absent, not just under-described |

## 100-Point Score

| Dimension | Weight | What To Evaluate |
|---|---:|---|
| Must-have fit | 30 | Required skills, years/scope, credentials, language, work mode |
| Role-relevant evidence | 20 | Directly relevant projects, outcomes, responsibilities, domain examples |
| Achievement strength | 15 | Metrics, scale, business impact, ownership, before/after clarity |
| Tooling and keywords | 15 | Tools, methods, platforms, terminology expected by the JD |
| Seniority and scope | 10 | Complexity, autonomy, leadership, cross-functional scope |
| Logistics and preference fit | 10 | Location, remote preference, salary band, industry preference |

## Match Labels

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---:|---|---|
| 80-100 | 冲 | 高匹配，建议尽快投递，并做岗位定制版简历。 |
| 65-79 | 稳 | 值得投，但需要针对 JD 补强表达或证据。 |
| 50-64 | 保 | 可以作为补充选择，最好配合内推、作品或强叙事。 |
| 0-49 | 不合适 | 不建议优先投入时间，除非有特殊战略原因。 |

## Evidence Tags

Use these tags in diagnosis:

- `Direct`: resume clearly proves the JD requirement.
- `Adjacent`: resume shows related evidence, but wording or context needs bridging.
- `Weak`: resume mentions it but lacks proof, scale, or outcome.
- `Missing`: no credible evidence in the provided resume.
- `Risk`: likely hard filter or interview failure point.

## Priority Decision

Recommend one of:

- `A`: apply within 24 hours, tailor resume, pursue referral if possible.
- `B`: apply after quick rewrite, no more than 30-45 minutes spent.
- `C`: only apply if the company/role is strategically valuable.
- `Skip`: do not spend time unless new evidence appears.

## What Counts As Fixable

Usually fixable through resume rewriting:

- JD keyword exists in experience but is not named.
- Impact exists but is buried or written as tasks.
- Relevant project is present but not ordered near the top.
- Summary is generic and not aimed at this role.

Not fixable through wording alone:

- Missing mandatory license, degree, or clearance.
- No evidence of the core job function.
- Seniority scope is far below role requirement.
- Tool or domain requirement is central and completely absent.
